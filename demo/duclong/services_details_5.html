THỦY ĐIỆM SÔNG LAM

Poliform - Italy is currently a leading player on the international furniture scene. The highly successful result of adventurous enterprise, the company has always based its vision on the search for quality by updating its lines in keeping with all that is good in contemporary lifestyle trends. Varenna kitchen is one of famous brand kitchen from Poliform since 1996.

Quality is a blend of superior design and uncompromising functionality. The Poliform|Varenna collection offers personalized solutions to suit contemporary tastes and styles. Elegant in form and highly flexible in design and detail, these high quality systems can be customized to complement your unique personality.

Emphasis on function is made without sacrificing the familiarity that creates a kitchen's warmth and liveliness. Build a kitchen you can live with... and in. Varenna delivers quality you can bring into your home.

Entrance to Vietnam end 2013 via RITA VO company, now Varenna showrooms offers to Vietnamese customers unique collections in kitchen. One of them is Artex seri.

Artex -A project signed by CR&S Varenna with a contemporary design and a natural aesthetics. Located at most beautiful ares of N1HOMEPRO HCM & HANOI, Artex is designed with elegance style.
