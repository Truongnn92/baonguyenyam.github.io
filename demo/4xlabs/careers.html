CAREERS

Be a part of the international 4xLabs family. Join our team!

Our users and money changers praise solutions from 4xLabs for their simplicity and efficiency. Because innovation and the continuous improvement of the user experience form the core of our DNA, we are always on the look out for new talent. We love individuals with an entrepreneurial mindset who aim high. We work hard, party hard and pay market rates.

If you like 4xLabs and think you can contribute to our mission, please send your resume to us at hr@4xlabs.co and tell us why in a few short sentences.

SALES

We are looking for sales people who will be accountable for building a network of money changer partners for Get4x / Biz4x and maintaining good relationships with them. You are comfortable meeting people, have strong empathy and are service-oriented.

PRODUCT & IT

We are looking for product managers and developers who will be tasked to design, develop, implement and maintain applications to support new business requirements. You will research and recommend new technologies, conducting testing and working in close-knit teams to deliver exciting products.

MARKETING

We are looking for interns who can be responsible for promoting the solutions of 4xLabs and driving widespread use of the apps through marketing, public relations and advertising. You should be a creative and highly resourceful individual.
