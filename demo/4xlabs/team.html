OUR TEAM

The success of 4xLabs is directly attributable to the passion, hard work and dedication of our diverse and international team. Over the last few years, we have grown from strength to strength, building on our core competencies, and varied experiences from different cultures and countries to launch the leading operating system for money changers in the region.

We are headquartered in Singapore, with a regional office in Vietnam and a network of employees across Asia Pacific. We place great importance on the personal and professional development of our people. Our executive team has a wealth of experience from finance, marketing and technology, and are key architects of the future of the cash exchange industry.

CHRIS VANDEN BERGHE

Chief Executive Officer

Chris has over 10 years of experience in financial technology through various roles with Thomson Reuters and Markit in London, Hong Kong and Singapore. He founded Nusquama, a regional remittance startup, in 2015. He loves the challenges of building a scalable business that leverages on cutting edge technology, especially if it solves a real customer problem. Chris holds a PhD in Computer Science (focus in Computer Security) with the University of Leuven, Belgium, and an EMBA from Columbia University. He likes to travel and enjoys going for hiking trips in his free time.
