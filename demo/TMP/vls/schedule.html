Start Dates 2012

Posted: Saturday,October 13, 2012

Thanks to the donation of VLS students, friends and families and VLS' effort in organizing the school visit, our charity brought a wealthy Mid-Autumn festival to 66 underprivileged children of Bà Xán, Bình Phước school in Cần Giờ district on September 29, 2012. 66 lanterns, 66 gift packages and a scholarship portion of 1,000,000VND were handed out. The total donation was valued up to 10 million VND. - 29 September 2012

It was a great Saturday with very nice weather, which was rare in this rainy season of Saigon. VLS students, friends and family members were excited to depart early to Binh Phuoc primary school, Bà Xán branch, in Cần Giờ district, Ho Chi Minh City. Bà Xán was the remote one out of the three branches of Binh Phuoc primary school, teaching 66 pupils. Most of them were very underprivileged. Our engaging visit generated great happiness not only for the children but also for all of us - multinational adults.

Two weeks earlier, VLS held the lantern making days. All of the participants could experience the creativity of Vietnamese cultural crafts which indeed sparked childhood memories. Everyone dedicatedly made a lantern for the sake of festival donation.
